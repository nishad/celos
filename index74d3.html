Dr. Ambika Nautiyal recieved PhD from Department of Physics, Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai, India  in Physics. At present she is at the post of Lecturer at the Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences.

  

AWARDS AND HONOURS

  • Senior Research fellowship (2008), awarded by Council of Scientific and Industrial Research [CSIR], India

  • Lecturer-ship qualification (2003)-awarded by Council of Scientific and Industrial Research [CSIR], India

  • Qualified in the Graduate Amplitude Test in Engineering [GATE] in Physics, with 93.72 percentile, 2003 

  

RESEARCH EXPERIENCE 

  • Design and development of picosecond non-collinear optical parametric amplifier

  • Design and development of femtosecond non-collinear optical parametric amplifier

  • Working experience with confocal microscopy

  • Hands on experience in the fluorescence microscopy

  • Measurement and analysis of lifetime decay of fluorophore

  

RESEARCH INTEREST

  • Ultrafast lasers 

  • Nonlinear optics

  • Time resolved fluorescence microscopy and spectroscopy

  • Optical parametric amplifiers and fluorescence up-conversion
