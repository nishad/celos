Dr. Sheenu Thomas recieved PhD from Cochin University of Science and Technolgy in Experimental Solid State Physics. At present she is at the post of Reader at the Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences. Her areas of interest are Solid State Physics, Optoelectronics and Chaclogenide glass based Photonics Materials.
