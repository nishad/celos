Module I - Fourier series and Fourier transform: properties of Fourier transform, convolution theorem, solving differential equations using Fourier transform technique

Module II - Complex variable: functions of complex variables, analytic functions, power series, Taylor and Laurent series, contour integration, Cauchy’s theorem, Cauchy’s integral formula, integration involving branch cuts and branch points

Module III - Group Theory: definition, multiplication table, permutation group, representation of group, symmetry group, isomorphism and homomorphism, character table, classes, symmetry operations, applications to simple polyatomic molecules like H2O

Module IV - Nonlinear differential equations: stability analysis, critical points, nonlinear oscillations, limit cycles, KdV equation, Sine Gordon equation, solitons

Module V - Theory of chaos and complex systems: logistic map, route to chaos, Lyapunov exponents, regular and strange attractors, generalized dimensions, characterization of chaotic system using time series, correlation dimension and K2 entropy
