Jaya Mathews joined CELOS in 2002. At present she is in ninth semester of Integrated MSc course in Photonics.

She is a native of Kottayam, Kerala. Her interest areas are Optical Sensors, Lasers and Optical Communication.. She wants to establish a R & D career in Photonics.

She did her sixth semester project on "Design and fabrication details of Nitrogen laser". She did herr final semester M.Sc main project titled "Expansion dynamics of Laser produced Lithium plasma in a transverse magnetic field "in the Institute for Plasma Research (IPR), Gandhinagar, Ahmedabad under the guidance of Dr.Ajai Kumar(Laser diagnostics group, IPR).

Now she is working as a Project associate in the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras (IIT-M), Chennai; on "supercontinuum generation based on the Nonlinear phenomena of the optical fiber".

She was a member of organising committee for PHOTONICS 2004 and PLASMA 2005.
