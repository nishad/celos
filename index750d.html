The university has provided hostel facility to the students and researchers of CELOS. The  registered students and researchers can apply for the university hostels.

The university has six hostels (3 for boys and 3 for girls)
Athulya (G)
Aishwarya (G)
Anushwara (G)
Sanathana (B)
Sarovar (B)
Siberia (B)
