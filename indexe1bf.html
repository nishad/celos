Reading section - Complex representation of light waves, interference of two monochromatic waves, interference by amplitude division and wavefront division, localized and nonlocalized fringes, Michelson interferometer, Fabry Perot interferometer

Module I - Interferometers: interference in a plane parallel plate and in a plate of varying thickness, Fizeau fringes, Mach-Zehnder interferometer, Sagnac interferometer, channeled spectra, achromatic fringes, fringes of equal thickness, fringes of equal chromatic order, broad band reflectors, interference filters, band pass filters, dichroic beam splitters and cold mirrors, measurements of standard length, speed of light and Michelson Morley experiment, interferometric measurements of rotation, detection of gravitational waves

Module II - Wavefront shearing interferometers: Twyman-Green interferometer, scanning Fabry-Perot interferometer, dynamic and static wavelength meters, fringe counting, heterodyne interferometry, digital interferometry
Adaptive optics: wavefront sensor, guided star systems, MEMS and deformable mirrors and wavefront corrections, actuators, adaptive optics and human eye

Module III - Grating spectrographs: various mounting techniques, resolution and dispersive power of spectrographs, single beam and double beam monochromators, UV-Vis-NIR and IR spectrophotometers, FTIR spectrophotometer, atomic absorption spectrophotometer, fluorometers

Module IV - Camera, high-speed camera, scanners, video camera, image projectors, LCD Projector, endoscopes

Module V - Telescopes: types of telescopes, optical telescopes, radio telescopes, RADAR and LIDAR
Microscopes: metallurgical microscopes, phase contrast microscope, confocal microscope, TEM and SEM
