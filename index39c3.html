Aneesh Alex did the course in the period 2002-2007.

He is a native of Thrissur, Kerala. His interest areas are Bio Photonics, Laser produced Plasma and Optical Image Processing. He wants to establish a successful career in the Photonics industry.

His sixth semester project was "Plasma generation using RF Oscillator". He presented a paper on this project at PLASMA 2006 held at Malaviya National Institute of Technology, Jaipur.

Currently he is a PhD student doing work in " Oral Cancer Diagnosis using Optical Coherence Tomography"  in Cardiff University, Britain.
