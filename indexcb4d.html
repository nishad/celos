The first batch of students graduated from the centre after completing their 5 year integrated MSc degree in Photonics in July 2007. The placements procured by the students of the first batch have set high standards for the coming batches.

Seven students have secured research positions at the leading universities abroad:
the Cardiff University - Wales
the University of Modena - Italy
the University of Camerino - Italy
the University of Dublin - Ireland
the University of Basel - Switzerland
the Technical University of Denmark
the City University of New York - USA

Three students have got research positions at leading research labs in India
Raman Research Institute - Bangalore
Plasma Research Laboratory - Gandhinagar
National Centre for Biological Sciences - Bangalore

The industrial placement has also been good with 3 students getting offshore placements in Sweden on a project by NeST Photonics and a student each getting placed at SAMEER (Society for Applied Microwave Electronics Engineering and Research),  TCS (Tata Consultancy Services) and  CTS (Cognizant Technology Solutions).

The second batch of students is graduated from the centre in April 2008. Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences is pleased to announce the commencement  of Placement Season 2008 for its second batch.

CELOS invites all the interested companies and research institutes to the campus to conduct a placement session for the passing out batch. The centre will make all the necessary arrangements to make your visit to the campus a success. Those organizations who cannot come to the campus can opt for an on-line or off-campus placement session.

All the interested organizations can visit the home page of Student Placement Office, CELOS for further details.
