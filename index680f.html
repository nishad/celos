Module I - Wave particle duality, Huygen’s principle, superposition of two sinusoidal waves, analytical and graphical methods, coherence
Interference by division of wave front: Young’s double slit experiment, Fresnel biprism, Newton’s rings in reflection and transmission mode, spatial and temporal coherence, optical beats

Module II - Interference by division of amplitude: Michelson interferometer, determination of wavelength separation and standardization of metre, Fabry Perot interferometer and Fabry Perot etalon, fringe visibility and resolving power of etalon, interference in thin films, colour of thin films, nonreflecting films, interference filters

Module III - Diffraction: Fresnel zones, rectilinear propagation of light and Fresnel theory, Theory of zone plate and its comparison with convex lens, Fraunhoffer and Fresnel Diffractions, Fresnel diffraction at straight edge, straight slit, Cornu’s Spiral, application of Cornu’s spiral in diffraction phenomena, Fraunhoffer diffraction at single slit, double slit and multiple slits
Moire patterns and its applications

Module IV - Theory of plane transmission grating: oblique and normal incidence, absent spectra, determination of wavelength of light using grating, dispersion and resolving power, blazed gratings, grating spectrometer, X-ray diffraction, grating spectrograph, different types of mountings

Module V - Polarization: experimental observation, polarization by reflection and refraction, Brewster angle, Malus laws, double refraction, uniaxial and biaxial crystals, optical activity, half shade polarimeter, quarter and half wave plates, production and detection of plane, elliptical and circular polarization of light, John’s vectors and matrices
Electro-optic magneto-optic and acousto optic effects (qualitative ideas)
