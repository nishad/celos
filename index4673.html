Module I - Origin of quantum theory, blackbody radiation, Planck’s law, Einstein’s photoelectric equations, Bohr atom model, Frank and Hertz experiment, Compton effect, Wilson-Sommerfeld quantization rule and its application to hydrogen atom, harmonic oscillator, rigid rotator, particle in a box, correspondence principle, Stern Gerlach experiment, matter waves, diffraction of electron beam

Module II - Time dependent and time independent Schrödinger equations, Schrödinger wave equation for free particle, superposition, wavepacket, Heisenberg’s uncertainty principle, interpretation of wavefunction, probability current density and continuity equation, expectation value, Ehrenfest theorem, stationary states and superposition principle

Module III - Hilbert space, orthonormal function, Dirac’s notation, linear operator, commutator bracket, eigen functions and eigen values, matrix representation of linear operator, Hermitian operator, Schmidt orthogonalization procedure, postulates of quantum mechanics, simultaneous measurability and general uncertainty relations, Schrödinger representation, Heisenberg’s representation and interaction representation
EPR paradox and Bell’s inequality (only introductory ideas)

Module IV - One dimensional square well potentials of finite and infinite depth, one dimensional potential barrier and alpha decay, Schrödinger wave equation and its solution for linear harmonic oscillator, box normalization of wave function, Dirac delta function

Module V - Particle in spherically symmetric potential, angular momentum operator, eigen value of L2, Hamiltonian of two interacting particles, rigid rotator, Hydrogen atom, energy eigen values, hydrogen wave function, radial probability density and hydrogen atom orbitals
