Bishwajeet Singh Bhardwaj is a 7th semester student of 5 Year Integrated M.Sc course in Photonics in Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences (CELOS). He joined CELOS in the 5th batch of the Integrated M.Sc program in July 2006 and is expected to complete the course by 2011.

List of Seminars taken by Bishwajeet as part of his course curriculum is as follows:

  •    Hydrodynamics 
  •    Nuclear reactor – critical condition, design aspects & classifications 
  •    Laser and its applications 
  •    Laser Tweezers
  •    Optical Biosensors
  •    Nanoparticle-based Optical Biosensors 

His primary academic interest is in Bio-Photonics and Nano-Photonics. His other interests involve Laser Physics, Experimental Condensed Matter, Plasmonics and Holography.

He has done ‘Synthesis of CdSe quantum dots and its characterisation’’ as a part of 6th semester project under M.Kailasnath, Lecturer, International School of Photonics as his supervisor.

He is a student member of the International Society for Optical Engineering (SPIE), Optical society of America (OSA), Laser & Electro-Optics Society (LEOS).

He is a native of Patna, Bihar.
