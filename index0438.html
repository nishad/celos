Dr. V. M. Nandakumaran received Ph.D. in 1979 from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore in theoretical condensed matter physics.

Currently he is a Professor at the International School of Photonics and Honorary Professor at  the Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences. His areas of interest are nonlinear dynamics and chaos, chaos and synchronisation of chaos in laser systems.

He is a life member Indian Physics Association, life member of Photonics Society of India and life member of Indian Complex System Society. He was the former director of International School of Photonics. He is also a visiting professor to the Department of Physics, Central University of Hyderabad, India.
