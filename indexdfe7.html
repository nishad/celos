Nikhil PK joined CELOS in 2004. At present he is in the 10th semester of the 5 Year Integrated MSc degree course in Photonics and would be completing his course in May 2009.

His areas of interest are Laser cooling, Optical Tweezer , Laser spectroscopy, Photonic crystal and Digital holography .

He has done mini project titled " Thin film preparation and Analysis " during the sixth semester of the course.
Currently he is doing his Xth semester project at Applied Optics Lab, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, in “Phase Unwrapping in Digital Holography ” under Dr. Joby Joseph.

During the course, he has undertaken seminars on:

* Chaos
* Cathode Ray Oscilloscope
* Magnetic Resonance Imaging
* Electro magnetic - bomb
* Fluroscence
* Thin film preparation and Analysis [Project seminar]
* Holographic memory

He was an active member in  the  coordination of  International conference; Photonics-2004, Cochin. He also worked actively for Annual Photonics Workshop 2005, Plasma 2005 conference held  at International school of Photonics.
