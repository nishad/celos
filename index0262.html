Reading Section - Maxwell’s equations, Poynting vector, propagation of EMW in conducting and nonconducting media, boundary conditions and Snell’s laws

Module I - Nonlinear optical coefficients, second order and third order susceptibility tensors, propagation of EMW through second order nonlinear media, OSHG, phase matching, OPO, frequency conversion, experimental technique to study second order nonlinearity

Module II - Third order NLO, FWM, OPC, TPA, stimulated Raman and Brillouin scattering, CARS, intensity dependent refractive index, SIT, self focusing, saturable and reverse saturable absorbers, optical limiting

Module III - TEM waves, lossless transmission lines, lossy transmission lines, EMW propagation in troposphere and ionosphere

Module IV - TE and TM waves in rectangular and circular wave guides, Q-factor, radiation from quarter wave monopole or half wave dipole

Module V - Microstrip transmission line, planar transmission lines, strip lines, microstrip lines, antenna structure, radiation pattern, antenna arrays


References:
1. Optical Electronics - A Yariv
2. Nonlinear Optics - Shen (Text)
3. Electromagnetic Waves and Radiating Systems - Jordan EC, Balmain (Text)
4. Introduction to Electromagnetism - Paul CA, Nassar S A
5. Antennas - Krauss J D (Text)
