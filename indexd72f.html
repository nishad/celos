Module I - General performance consideration of transmission lines, coaxial cables, wave guides, microstrip, stripline, optical fibre, comparison of losses, Radio frequency beam forming, scanning techniques, circuit beam formers, imaging reflector antennas, RF electronic beam steering techniques for optical fibres

Module II - Phased array antennas, AO time delays for phased array antennas, delay line, time delays arrangements for phased array antennas, experimental results, two dimensional array antennas, beam steering

Module III - Optically controlled beam scanning, phase shifters, injection locking, beam steering using subharmonic injection locking, interjunction locking method, unilateral injection locking

Module IV - Binary optical delay lines, fibre optical delay lines, square root cascade delay lines, back ground binary fibre optical delay line, architecture and design of BIFODEL

Module V - Optical beam steering of antennas using lasers, Optically controlled array, image mask, fibre optic shutter switch, signal to noise ratio and laser out put, radiation pattern and reduction in side lobe level, photonic crystals for microwave applications


References:
1. Antenna Design with Fibre Optics - A Kumar (Text)
2. Coplanar Waveguides, Circuits, Components and Systems - E J Simons
3. Integrated Optical Circuits - E J Murphy
4. Photonics Crystals - S G Johnson, J D Joannopoulos
