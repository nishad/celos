Module I - Nano materials: nanocrystals, quantum dots and quantum wells, nanocrystals of III-V compounds and indirect gap materials, energy states of quantum dots, photonics applications of quantum dots and quantum wells, photonic switches and modulators using quantum dots and quantum wells

Module II - Organic materials for photonics, evaluation of second order and third order optical nonlinearities, organic materials for second and third order nonlinear optics, photorefractive polymers, polymers for light emitting sources, optical limiting, polymers for optical fiber

Module III - Sol-Gel materials for photonics applications, method of preparations, electro optic, magneto optic and acousto optic materials, photonic devices based on EO, MO, AO effects, Fluoride glass based fibres and their applications

Module IV - Thin film optics based components: design and production of thin films, anti reflection and dichroic reflection coatings, DWDM filters, production and characterization of optical thin films, PLD, CVD, PVD, MBE, dip, spin and spray coatings

Module V - Optical IC and wave guide structures: coplanar waveguides, frequency doublers, mixers, MEMS, Photonic band gap structures, waveguides of elevated and buried structures, optical ICs- architecture and applications, CD read/write mechanism, materials and production


References:
1. Optical Properties of Semiconductor Quantum Dots - V Woggon (Text)
2. Nonlinear Optics of Organic Molecules and Polymers - H S Nalwa, S Miyata (Text)
3. Sol-Gel for Photonics - B J Thompson (Text)
4. Practical Design and Production of Optical Thin Films - Marcel & Dekker (Text)
5. Coplanar Waveguide Circuits, Components and Systems - R N Simons (Text)
