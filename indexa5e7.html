Dr. P. Radhakrishnan received Ph.D. degree from Cochin University of Science and Technology in 1986.

Currently he is a Professor and at the International School of Photonics, Cochin University of Science and Technology. He was also the immediate past Director of ISP. He has also been a lecturer at the Cochin College from 1979 to 1988. His research interests include laser technology, laser spectroscopy, and fiber optic sensors.

He is the present President of the Photonics Society of India and life member of Indian Laser Association, Indian Association of Physics Teachers and the Indian Physics Association.
