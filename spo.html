Student's Placement Office - Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences


The Student's Placement Office holds the responsibility of overseeing the annual placement exercise of Five Year Integrated MSc Photonics students of Centre of Excellence in Lasers and Optoelectronic Sciences.

We usually prefer on-campus placement sessions but can make arrangements for off-campus or on-line placement sessions if desired by the potential recruiters.

The activities of Student's Placement Office are as follows:
  1. Contacting various Photonics companies and research institutions regularly to invite them to the centre for recruitment.
  2. Assisting the potential recruiters in conducting various tests, interviews and GDs.
  3. Helping the final semester students to get projects in various Photonics companies and research institutes.
  4. Organizing industrial visits to various industries and research establishments.
  5. Inviting experts from the Photonics industry to take seminars on the latest trends.
