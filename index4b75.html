Jenu Varghese Chacko joined CELOS in 2004. At present he is in the eighth semester of the 5 Year Integrated MSc degree course in Photonics and would be completing his course in May 2009.

His areas of interest are Remote Sensing, Laser Systems, Laser Ablated Plasma, Quantum Computing ,Optical Computing and  Energy crisis solutions.

He has done theoretical mini project titled " Two photon Interference problem : Quantum Optics" during the sixth semester of the course.

During the course, he has undertaken seminars on:

* Holography
* Remote Sensing
* Infrared Imaging
* Quantum Computing
* Remote sensing II
* Quintessence & QAD
* Mystism in Physics
* Theoretical study of two photon and three photon interference

He is a student member of the International Society for Optical Engineering (SPIE), the Optical Society of America (OSA) and the Photonics Society of India (PSI). He is the acting vice president of SPIE-ISP Student chapter working in International School of Photonics, Cochin University.

He is the Placement Coordinator of CELOS for the Placement Season - 2008. He is the Website Administrator of CELOS during 2007 - 08.
