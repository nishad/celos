Aasif Muhammed joined CELOS in 2002 and passed in 2007.

He is a native of Cochin itself. His interest areas are Display Devices, Optical Instrumentation and Optical Communication. He wants to establish a successful career in the industry. He has good computer knowledge and possess excellent programming skills.

For his sixth semester project he designed a "Human Pulse Counter" and the tenth semester main project was titled as " Face recognition using eigen faces " .

He was an active participant in various activities of the department as well as ISP-SPIE student chapter. Also, he was in the organizing committee of PLASMA 2005 and Annual Photonics Workshop 2005 and 2006.

He is also associated with many cultural activities and has won prizes at the university level.
