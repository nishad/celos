Jithin Jose joined CELOS in 2002. At present he is in ninth semester of Integrated MSc course in Photonics.

He is a native of Thrissur, Kerala. His interest areas are Bio Photonics, Laser produced Plasma and Remote Sensing.. He wants to establish a successful career in the Photonics industry.

His sixth semester project was "Plasma generation using RF Oscillator". He presented a paper on this project at PLASMA 2006 held at Malaviya National Institute of Technology, Jaipur.

He is an active participant in various activities of the department as well as ISP-SPIE student chapter. He was a member of organising committee for PHOTONICS 2004, PLASMA 2005 and Indo-UK Workshop 2006 for Fibre Optics and Applications.
