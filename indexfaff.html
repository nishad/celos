Bejoy Varghese joined CELOS in 2002. At present he is in ninth semester of Integrated MSc course in Photonics.

He is a native of Thrissur, Kerala. He is interested in Theoretical Physics. Areas of interest being Quantum Optics and Chaos. He wants to establish a research career in Theoretical Physics.

His sixth semester project was on "Coupled Logistic Maps and Synchronization Properties".

He is an active participant in various activities of the department as well as ISP-SPIE student chapter. He was in the organizing committee of PHOTONICS 2004 and PLASMA 2005. He is also an active member in the Open House programmes of the ISP-SPIE chapter.
