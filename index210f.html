The CELOS computer centre is one of the most modern computing centres of the university. With its 2 HP workstations and 30 latest HP personal computers, the centre caters to the CELOS members intrested in computing, simulation and programming.

Location: The CELOS computer centre is located in Physics building.

Opening Hours: 10.00 AM - 4 PM on university working days; closed on Sundays.

Membership: Membership of the computer centre is open to all registered students, researchers and staff of CELOS..

Internet: The CELOS computer centre is connected to the campus wide fiber optic LAN of the University, providing high speed internet connectivity.

CELOS Members: CELOS members are also allowed to use the computing facility at the CUSAT Computer Centre. Members must register separately in that centre.
