The basic admission procedure for 5 Year Integrated M.Sc in Photonics is the All India Combined Entrance Test (CAT) conducted by CUSAT. Usually the notification for the CAT is issued in January / February and it is conducted in April / May.

For exact details visit the website:
http://www.cusat.nic.in

The eligibilty for getting admission in 5 Year Integrated M.Sc in Photonics is pass in +2 examinations with a minimum of 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.
