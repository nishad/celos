Module I - Linear atom optics: light forces on atoms, atomic cooling, Doppler and Sisyphus cooling, evaporative cooling, atomic beam collimation and focusing, channeling by standing waves, evanescent field mirrors, focused laser beam mirrors

Module II - Atomic diffraction: Ramn-Nath and Bragg regime, grating and interferometers, atomic traps and cavities, magneto optic, magnetic and optical traps, atomic waveguides

Module III - Quantum atom optics: matter wave coherence, Bose-Einstein condensation, experiments in alkali vapors, atom lasers, matter wave solitons

Module IV - Nonlinear wave mixing: Atomic four wave mixing, mixing of optical and matter waves, parametric amplification of atomic and optical fields

Module V - Entanglement between atomic and optical fields, matter waves super radiance, matter wave amplification, application of atom optics
